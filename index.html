A DENOMINATION-FREE, GOSPEL-CENTERED, MISSIONAL CHURCH FOR EVERY MAJOR CITY IN SOUTH ASIA

The primary focus of GROW Gospel Initiatives is to start Gospel-Centered churches in the major cities of south Asia. These churches emphasize God’s love that is demonstrated through the death, burial, and resurrection of Jesus Christ over moralism and legalism. Man does not have the inherent ability to measure up to God’s standard unless God continuously enables him through the work of the Holy Spirit. Therefore, these churches help their members to shift their emphasis away from their ownself on to the Triune God.

INTERNSHIP FOR NEW CHURCH INITIATIVE

GROW Gospel Initiatives offers a one-year church planting internship to a limited number of selected candidates. Prior to the beginning of their internship, the selected candidates need to undergo a personality test and internship profile. The candidate needs to be married, have an undergraduate degree from a university and a Bible degree from a reputed seminary, and have a good standing with a local church. Selected candidates will receive a stipend during their internship. For further details, please contact.

SHORT TERM MISSION TRIPS TO SOUTH ASIA

GROW Gospel Initiatives organizes short term mission trips to south Asian countries from North and South America, Europe, Japan, and South Korea for education, exposure, and participation. For further Details, please contact.
